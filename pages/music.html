Music

The Marías is an indie pop band that formed in Los Angeles in 2016. Made up of Puerto Rican singer María Zardoya and drummer Josh Conway, their style combines the charisma of pop, the subtlety of soul and a significant strength. feminine that underlies its melodies. In their live shows they are joined as guest musicians by Jesse Perlman on guitar and Edward James on keyboard to consolidate the vibrant energy of their sound proposal. Cinema is the name of this material that, according to The Marías, relates to its beginnings. For the title they decided to choose a simple word that would read as well in both Spanish and English - languages ​​that The Marías implements in their lyrics. An important point to emphasize since it has become part of their essence and could not be missing on this LP. Now, it is time for you to allow this movie to start. Close your eyes and enjoy it.
